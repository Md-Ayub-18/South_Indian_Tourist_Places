God's Own Country, where serene backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage create paradise on earth

The commercial capital of Kerala, known for its Chinese fishing nets, historic fort, and beautiful backwaters. A blend of traditional and modern influences.

Picturesque hill station famous for its sprawling tea plantations, misty mountains, and diverse wildlife including the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.

Famous as the "Venice of the East" for its intricate network of backwaters, houseboats, and serene canals that offer a unique travel experience.
