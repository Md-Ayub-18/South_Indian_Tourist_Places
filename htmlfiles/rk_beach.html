RK Beach - The Jewel of Vizag's Coastline

A picturesque beachfront offering stunning views of the Bay of Bengal and vibrant city life

About RK Beach

RK Beach, officially known as Ramakrishna Beach, is one of the most popular and beautiful beaches in Visakhapatnam. Named after the spiritual leader Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa, this beach stretches along the Bay of Bengal and offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, recreational activities, and cultural attractions.

The beach is famous for its long coastline, golden sands, and the picturesque view of the Dolphin's Nose Hill in the distance. It's a favorite spot for both locals and tourists to relax, enjoy water sports, and witness spectacular sunrises and sunsets over the Bay of Bengal.

Location

Beach Road, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Length

Approximately 5 kilometers

Water Activities

Swimming, Jet Skiing, Banana Boat Rides

Best Time to Visit

October to March (pleasant weather)

Attractions and Landmarks

RK Beach is surrounded by several important landmarks. The INS Kurusura Submarine Museum, a real submarine converted into a museum, is a major attraction. The Victory at Sea War Memorial, dedicated to soldiers who died in the 1971 Indo-Pak war, stands as a solemn reminder of India's naval history.

The beach also features the Kali Temple, a popular religious site, and the Park Hotel area which offers various food stalls and entertainment options. The beautifully maintained beach road is perfect for evening strolls and cycling, with dedicated pathways for pedestrians and cyclists.

Recreational Activities

RK Beach offers numerous recreational activities for visitors. The beach is ideal for swimming, though visitors should be cautious and swim only in designated areas. Various water sports like jet skiing, banana boat rides, and speed boating are available for adventure enthusiasts.

The beachfront has well-maintained gardens, children's play areas, and seating arrangements. Evening time sees the beach come alive with food stalls selling local delicacies, street performers, and artists. The musical fountain show in the evening is a major attraction for families.

Sunrise and Sunset Views

RK Beach is renowned for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views. The sight of the sun rising from the waters of the Bay of Bengal is particularly spectacular. Many morning walkers and photographers visit the beach early to capture this beautiful moment.

Similarly, the sunset views are equally mesmerizing, with the sky turning into hues of orange, pink, and purple. The cool sea breeze and the sound of waves make these moments truly magical and romantic, making it a popular spot for couples and families alike.

Visitor Information

RK Beach is accessible 24 hours, but the best time to visit is during early morning (5:00 AM to 8:00 AM) for sunrise views and evening (4:00 PM to 8:00 PM) for sunset views and recreational activities. The beach has clean restroom facilities, changing rooms, and shower areas.

There are numerous food stalls and restaurants nearby offering local Andhra cuisine, seafood, and snacks. Visitors should be cautious while swimming and follow safety instructions. Lifeguards are present during peak hours, and it's advisable to swim only in designated areas marked by flags.
