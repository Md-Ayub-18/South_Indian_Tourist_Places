Discover Andhra Pradesh

Visakhapatnam

The Jewel of the East Coast, known for its beautiful beaches, serene harbors, and lush green hills. Home to INS Kurusura Submarine Museum and Yarada Beach.

Tirupati

Spiritual capital of Andhra Pradesh, famous for the Sri Venkateswara Temple atop Tirumala Hills. One of the most visited pilgrimage centers in the world.

Amaravati

The capital city with rich Buddhist heritage, known for the Amaravati Stupa and as an ancient Buddhist settlement. A blend of historical significance and modern development.

Picturesque hill station known for its coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture. Famous for the Borra Caves and scenic train journey through Eastern Ghats.
